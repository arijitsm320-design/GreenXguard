Our Story

GreenXguard was established to bridge the gap between sustainability and legal accountability. In an era where "greenwashing" has become a significant risk, we recognized that many Indian companies struggle to navigate complex environmental regulations like the CCPA 2024 guidelines.

We serve as a private verification body that helps businesses validate their sustainability promises, ensuring they build genuine trust while avoiding heavy legal penalties.

Our Mission

"To empower brands with the transparency and legal verification needed to make authentic sustainability claims, thereby eliminating greenwashing and fostering a more honest green economy."
